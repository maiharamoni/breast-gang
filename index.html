No woman should die due to lack of information about Breast Cancer

What We Fight For

Help all women to know the risks


So that every woman, at any age, understands what factors increase her chances of developing breast cancer and knows what to do to manage those risks.

Help all women know how to act


So that they know where, when and how to seek help and medical advice whenever necessary.

Help all women recognize the signs


So that they know what is “normal” in relation to the health of the breasts and what deserves attention so that they can quickly identify the first signs of the disease.
